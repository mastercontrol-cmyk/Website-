PROJECT TITLE

Guardian — Personal AI Safety Agent

OBJECTIVE

Create a cinematic, high-end product showcase website for a startup called Guardian, launching first in Chennai, India.

The website must feel:

Premium

Calm

Intelligent

Safety-first

Technologically advanced

Globally scalable

This is not a simple landing page.
It is an immersive product narrative experience.

CORE CONCEPT

Guardian is:

A personal AI safety agent that lives on your devices, watches over you 24/7, learns your patterns, connects to your services, and acts proactively in the digital and physical world — with permanent safety boundaries.

It is not a chatbot.
It is not a panic button.
It is a presence.
